Optimal PDF Export Settings

To export a stunning, pixel-perfect PDF, please configure your browser print dialog settings as follows:

  1. Destination: Save as PDF (or Microsoft Print to PDF)
  2. Layout: Portrait
  3. Paper Size: A4
  4. Margins: None (critical to align custom headers & footers)
  5. Scale: Default (or 100%)
  6. Options: Check "Background graphics" (crucial for printing colors & patterns)
  7. Options: Uncheck "Headers and footers" (removes browser page dates/file paths)

💡 Make sure "Background graphics" is enabled, otherwise images and colored headers will not print!
